Pulling out common factors.

\subsection*{Method 2.}
Using identities.
\subsubsection*{The Quadratic Identity}
\[a^2 \pm 2ab + b^2 = (a\pm b)^2\]
\subsubsection*{The Conjugate Identity}
\[a^2 - b^2 = (a + b)(a - b)\]
